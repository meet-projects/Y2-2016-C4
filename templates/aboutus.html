PicPeace is webapp which is basically a photos app, where you can see pictures from both Israel and Palestine, You enter to the main page and there are categories like activities, food, education, where you enter and see two pictures beside each other without any caption or description just five basic questions, you answer them and then click on the submit button You see the percentages of what people from both nationalities answered, and we say which picture belonged to which country, and a known stereotype about one of the countries and try to fix people perspective about it and an explanation of what we meant in the pictures.
